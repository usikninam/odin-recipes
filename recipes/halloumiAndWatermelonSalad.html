Halloumi and watermelon salad

Description

This vibrant Halloumi and Watermelon Salad is a refreshing and savory-sweet dish that's perfect for warm weather. Juicy watermelon combines beautifully with the salty, golden halloumi and a touch of heat from red chilli. Finished with a mint garnish and a tangy white balsamic dressing, it's a delightful salad to share with friends and family.

The recipe serves approximately 8 people and is quick to prepare, making it an ideal option for summer gatherings or light meals.

Ingredients

  • 500g watermelon, peeled and cut into thin wedges
  • 2 x 250g packs halloumi, thinly sliced
  • 1 red chilli, finely chopped
  • A handful of mint leaves, shredded
  • 2 tbsp white balsamic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Place the watermelon in a large dish. Whisk together the white balsamic and olive oil with a pinch of sea salt, then stir in the chopped chilli. Pour the dressing over the watermelon, toss gently, and let it sit for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Dry-fry the halloumi slices on each side until they are golden brown.
  3. On a serving platter, layer the marinated watermelon and fried halloumi. Pour any remaining dressing from the watermelon dish over the layered salad. Scatter shredded mint leaves on top and serve immediately.
